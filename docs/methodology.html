Scope

This project is a curated directory of top AI companies, top engineering companies, and product-first technology organizations that are highly relevant for software, data, ML, platform, and infrastructure study.

Default inclusion bar

  • company is relevant to engineering, AI, infrastructure, developer tools, or technical product innovation
  • company usually has 500+ employees
  • company has clear product, engineering, or research relevance
  • company facts can be supported with official public sources
  • engineering blog, AI blog, or technical publication links are included when available

What this is not

  • not a formal financial ranking
  • not a complete list of all technology companies
  • not a claim that every company is equally strong in every engineering area

Taxonomy

The current information architecture uses:

  1. business type
  2. sub-type
  3. company

This keeps the D3 treemap legible while allowing deeper filtering through search, country, and tags.

Treemap sizing

Version 1 uses equal company tile weights. This avoids inventing a score before a transparent ranking methodology exists. The directory is better understood as a study map than a rigid leaderboard.

Data quality

Each record is designed to contain a stable id, classification, country of origin, key facts, and official source links. Validation checks cover unique ids, required fields, HTTPS URLs, and taxonomy alignment.
